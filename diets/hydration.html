The Importance of Hydration

Staying hydrated is one of the most important things you can do for your health. Water is essential for numerous bodily functions, including regulating temperature, transporting nutrients, and lubricating joints.

Tips for Staying Hydrated:

  • Carry a reusable water bottle with you throughout the day.
  • Drink water before, during, and after exercise.
  • Eat fruits and vegetables with high water content, like watermelon and cucumber.
  • Listen to your body; if you feel thirsty, drink water.
