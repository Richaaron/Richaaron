Cardio Blast

This high-intensity cardio workout is designed to get your heart rate up and burn a serious number of calories. Get ready to sweat!

Exercises:

  • Jumping Jacks: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • High Knees: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • Burpees: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Mountain Climbers: 3 sets of 30 seconds
