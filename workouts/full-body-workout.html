Full Body Workout

This routine is designed to target all your major muscle groups in a single session. It's perfect for beginners and those looking for an efficient workout.

Exercises:

  • Squats: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Rows: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Overhead Press: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Deadlifts: 3 sets of 8 reps
